About Harringtons

Harringtons Solicitors are lawyers based in the West Midlands working online and from offices in Sutton Coldfield and Tamworth. We specialise in Criminal Defence and Private Client Services in Family and Children's matters. Our clients' ask us to work all over the Country.

Our Criminal Defence work takes us into Police Stations, Regulatory Offices, Tribunals and Courts accross the UK. If you are being investigated or have to appear before a Tribunal or go to Court we have the experience to help you get through that ordeal.

Our Private Client team advise client's in all areas of family law including probate and changes of name. Whether drawing up a pre-marital agreement, dealing with family and relationship breakdown or a financial settlement that cares about your rights and those of your children we care about giving you the best advice.
